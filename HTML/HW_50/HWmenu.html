Black Ivory Coffee

With a 2017 vintage of only 265 lbs, Black Ivory Coffee is the world's rarest and most expensive coffee. Naturally refined by rescued elephants. A delicate yet complex cup with notes of dark chocolate, malt, hint of grass, spice and almost tea-like without the burnt or bitter taste of regular coffee. Enjoy this solo pack's treasures of taste. You can be one of the few to experience the world's rarest and most expensive coffee, while giving back to the elephants who help to make it. A portion of the proceeds go back to The elephants!
